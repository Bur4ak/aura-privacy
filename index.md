Privacy Policy
Last Updated: May 6, 2026

Burak Yıldırım ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Aura mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.
1. Information We Collect
Account Information
When you create an account, we collect:

Email address
Username (display name)
Password (encrypted, we cannot see it)
Location Data
We collect your precise location (GPS) to allow you to leave and discover Auras at physical locations.
Location data is used only while the App is in use and is not tracked in the background.
User-Generated Content
Text messages ("Auras") you create, including the emoji and location coordinates associated with them.
Votes (upvotes/downvotes) you cast on other users' Auras.
Device and Usage Data
Device type and operating system version
App usage analytics (crash reports, performance data)
Advertising identifiers (for personalized ads, with your consent)
2. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected information to:

Provide and maintain the App's core functionality (creating, discovering, and voting on Auras)
Authenticate your identity and secure your account
Display location-based content on the map and in AR view
Process in-app purchases
Show advertisements (for free users)
Improve the App's performance and user experience
Enforce our Terms of Service and prevent abuse
3. Third-Party Services
We use the following third-party services:
Firebase (Google)
Firebase Authentication: For account creation and login
Cloud Firestore: For storing Auras, user profiles, and votes
Cloud Functions: For secure server-side operations
Firebase Storage: For storing user-uploaded photos (when enabled)
Privacy Policy: https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy
Google AdMob
Used to display advertisements to free users
May collect device identifiers and usage data for ad personalization
Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
RevenueCat
Used to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions
Processes purchase transactions and subscription status
Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy
4. Data Storage and Security
Your data is stored on Firebase servers located in Europe (europe-west3 region).
We use industry-standard security measures including encrypted connections (HTTPS/TLS) and Firebase Security Rules to protect your data.
Passwords are handled by Firebase Authentication and are never stored in plain text.
5. Data Retention
Your account data and Auras are retained as long as your account is active.
When you delete your account, all your data (profile, Auras, votes, and uploaded photos) is permanently deleted.
You can delete your account at any time from the Profile section in the App.
6. Your Rights
You have the right to:

Access your personal data through your profile in the App
Delete your account and all associated data at any time
Opt out of personalized advertising through your device settings
Export your data by contacting us
7. Children's Privacy
The App is rated 9+ and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, please contact us and we will delete it.
8. Advertising
Free users see rewarded advertisements to access scanning features.
Premium subscribers do not see advertisements.
You can control ad personalization through your iOS device settings under Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.
9. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Last Updated" date.
