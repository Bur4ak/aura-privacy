--- layout: default title: Privacy Policy --- # Privacy Policy **Last Updated:** May 6, 2026 Burak Yıldırım ("we", "our", or "us") operates the **Aura** mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App. --- ### 1. Information We Collect #### Account Information When you create an account, we collect: * **Email address** * **Username** (display name) * **Password** (encrypted, we cannot see it) #### Location Data * We collect your **precise location (GPS)** to allow you to leave and discover Auras at physical locations. * Location data is used only while the App is in use and is not tracked in the background. #### User-Generated Content * Text messages ("Auras") you create, including the emoji and location coordinates associated with them. * Votes (upvotes/downvotes) you cast on other users' Auras. #### Device and Usage Data * Device type and operating system version. * App usage analytics (crash reports, performance data). * Advertising identifiers (for personalized ads, with your consent). --- ### 2. How We Use Your Information We use the collected information to: * Provide and maintain the App's core functionality. * Authenticate your identity and secure your account. * Display location-based content on the map and in AR view. * Process in-app purchases and show advertisements. * Improve performance and prevent abuse. --- ### 3. Data Storage and Security * Your data is stored on **Firebase servers** located in Europe (`europe-west3` region). * We use industry-standard security measures including **HTTPS/TLS encryption**. * Passwords are handled by Firebase Authentication and are never stored in plain text. --- ### 5. Data Retention & Deletion * Your data is retained as long as your account is active. * **Account Deletion:** When you delete your account, all your data (profile, Auras, votes, and photos) is **permanently deleted**. * You can delete your account at any time from the **Profile** section in the App. --- ### 6. Your Rights You have the right to: * Access your personal data through the App. * Delete your account at any time. * Opt out of personalized advertising via device settings. --- ### 7. Children's Privacy The App is rated **9+** and is not directed at children under 13. If we discover we have collected data from a child under 13, we will delete it immediately. --- ### 8.
